CERES BUS

DESCRIPTION:

Ceres Bus operates in Tanjay City, which is located in the province of Negros Oriental in the Visayas region of the Philippines. In Tanjay City, Ceres Bus has a bus terminal that serves as a major hub for transportation to and from the city.

The Ceres Bus terminal in Tanjay City is located in Barangay Sta. Cruz and is easily accessible from the city center. From the terminal, passengers can take buses to various destinations in Negros Oriental and nearby provinces, such as Bacolod, Dumaguete, and Cebu.

Ceres Bus in Tanjay City operates a range of buses, including air-conditioned coaches and non-aircon buses, to cater to the diverse needs of its passengers. The company provides regular bus services with scheduled departures throughout the day, as well as point-to-point services for faster travel to specific destinations.

The fares for Ceres Bus services in Tanjay City are generally affordable, and the company offers discounts to senior citizens, students, and persons with disabilities. Passengers can also choose between different seating options, such as regular and premium seats, depending on their preferences and budget.

Overall, Ceres Bus in Tanjay City is a convenient and reliable option for travelers and commuters looking for affordable transportation in Negros Oriental and the Visayas region of the Philippines.
