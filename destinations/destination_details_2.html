TANJAY BOULEVARD

DESCRIPTION:

"Tanjay City Boulevard" or the "Tanjay Boulevard." The boulevard is a seaside promenade that stretches along the Tanjay Bay, offering a scenic view of the sea and the nearby islands. The boulevard is a popular spot for locals and visitors to stroll, jog, or bike while enjoying the cool sea breeze. There are also several restaurants and food stalls along the boulevard that offer fresh seafood and local delicacies. At night, the boulevard is lit up with colorful lights, creating a festive and romantic atmosphere.
