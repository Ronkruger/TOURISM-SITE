LUPARAN FALLS

DESCRIPTION:

Luparan Falls is a popular tourist attraction located in Barangay Salngan, Tanjay City. It is a series of cascading waterfalls that flows into a clear pool of water, surrounded by lush greenery and rock formations. To get to Luparan Falls, visitors need to trek for about 30 minutes from the jump-off point. The trail can be quite challenging and rocky, so it's important to wear appropriate footwear and bring enough water. Once you reach the falls, you can take a refreshing dip in the cool waters or relax by the poolside while enjoying the scenic view.
